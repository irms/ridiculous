Support: FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

-Has Zoo Escape been released yet? Where can I get it?

Zoo Escape is scheduled for release on May 11th. It can be found in the downloads page of this web-site, free of charge.

-What will Zoo Escape be released on?

Zoo Escape will be available on the PC only.

-WIll Zoo Escape be in multiple languages?

Zoo Escape is currently English only. Since it is open-source, someone may translate it in the future.

-What kind of game is Zoo Escape?

Zoo Escape is an adventure/puzzle game. Playing as a penguin, the objective is to break free from Boulder Zoo before dawn next day."

Is this video game online?

A LAN or internet connection is necessary in order to play. A player plays from an outside client, wherein data is transmitted to a central server to process your scores. This is then retransmitted back to you in the form of results/scores for you to see."

Who made Zoo Escape?

Zoo Escape was made by the Interactive Game Design Lab at the Center for Advanced Research and Technology based in Fresno, California.
